Our Mission

The Liberation War is a non-profit initiative aimed at preserving the history of the 1971 Bangladesh Liberation War for future generations. Our mission is to create a comprehensive and accessible online resource that honors the sacrifices made and ensures these stories are never forgotten. We collect, catalog, and display historical documents, personal interviews, and rare photographs related to the war. This archive serves as an educational tool for researchers, students, and anyone interested in understanding the birth of Bangladesh.
