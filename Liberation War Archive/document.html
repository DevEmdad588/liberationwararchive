The Proclamation of Independence
  • Date: April 17, 1971
  • Source: Provisional Government of Bangladesh

The Proclamation of Independence is a historic document that declared the independence of Bangladesh from Pakistan on April 17, 1971. It was issued by the Provisional Government of Bangladesh, also known as the Mujibnagar Government, which was formed in exile during the Bangladesh Liberation War.

Indo-Soviet Treaty of Peace, Friendship and Cooperation
  • Date: August 9, 1971
  • Source: Governments of India and USSR

The Indo-Soviet Treaty of Peace, Friendship and Cooperation was a bilateral treaty signed between India and the Soviet Union on August 9, 1971. The treaty aimed to strengthen the relationship between the two countries and promote peace and stability in the region.
